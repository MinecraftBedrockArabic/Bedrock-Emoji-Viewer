## [1.1.0] - 2025-12-25

### Added
- Pressing `Ctrl + Shift + U` after typing `0xXXXX`, `\uXXXX`, `U+XXXX`, or `XXXX` inserts the corresponding emoji character.
- Typing `0xXX`, `\uXX`, `U+XX`, or `XX` and pressing `Ctrl + Shift + U` opens a picker showing all emojis from the matching `glyph_XX.png` for selection and insertion.
- Updated the extensions logo

---

## [1.0.0] - 2025-12-23

### Added
- Initial release
- Inline display of Bedrock emoji glyphs
- Hover info for Unicode characters
- Automatic glyph detection in workspace font folders
- Vanilla emoji fallback support (`glyph_E0.png`, `glyph_E1.png`)
- Setting to show or hide the original unicode character
